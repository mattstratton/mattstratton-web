The Sales Org Isn't (Totally) Evil
No matter what your job is, you'd do it better if you
understand how your company makes money. And you'll do
that even better if you understand the sales function in your
org.
There are toxic people who work in sales. There are also
AEs and SAs who know more about how our technology
products and organizations work than a lot of the
detractors do.
Getting to know how sales and marketing work (and heaven
forbid, collaborating with those folks) might teach you a
thing or two about your products and users.
