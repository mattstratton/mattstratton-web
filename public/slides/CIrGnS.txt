Observations
What I observe (see, hear, remember,
imagine...free from my evaluations) that does
or does not contribute to my well-being:
"When I (see, hear) ..."
What you observe (see, hear, remember,
imagine...free from my evaluations) that does
or does not contribute to your well-being:
"When you (see, hear) ..."
@mattstratton

Feelings
How I feel (emotion or sensation rather than
thought) in relation to what I observe:
"I feel ..."
How you feel (emotion or sensation rather than
thought) in relation to what you observe:
"You feel ..."
@mattstratton

Needs
What I need or value (rather than a preference
or a speciÞc action) that causes my feelings:
"... because I need/value ..."
What you need or value (rather than a
preference or a speciÞc action) that causes
your feelings:
"... because you need/value ..."
@mattstratton

Requests
• Clearly requesting that which would enrich my life without demanding
• Empathically receiving that which would enrich your life without hearing
any demand
@mattstratton

Requests
The concrete actions I would like taken:
"Would you be willing to ...?"
The concrete actions you would like taken:
"Would you like ...?"
