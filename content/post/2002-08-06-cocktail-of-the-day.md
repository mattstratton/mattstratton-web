**Alabama Slammer**
  
Fill Collins glass with ice.
  
Pour
  
1/2 oz Vodka
  
1/2 oz Amaretto
  
1/2 oz Strawberry Liqueur
  
1/2 oz Southern Comfort
  
1/2 oz Sloe Gin
  
Fill with orange juice.
