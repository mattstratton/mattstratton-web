abstractHtml: |
  <p>So you’ve had an incident. Restoring service is just the first step; your team should also be prepared to learn from incidents and outages. In this workshop, you will learn some best practices around postmortems/incident reviews to help your team and organization see incidents as a learning opportunity and not just a disruption in service.</p>
  <p>In this workshop, attendees will:</p>
  <ul>
  <li>Get an overview of blameless postmortems</li>
  <li>Learn techniques for effective information sharing</li>
  <li>Learn how to run a postmortem meeting effectively</li>
  <li>Understand the difference between “blame” and “accountability”</li>
  <li>Step through a real-world postmortem that occurred at a major SaaS organization</li>
  </ul>
